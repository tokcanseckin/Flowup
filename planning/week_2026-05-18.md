## Watch-outs

1. **Email deliverability** — Gmail/Outlook will mark you as spam until you configure SPF, DKIM, DMARC records. Use a transactional email service (SendGrid, Postmark) instead of raw SMTP for production.
2. **VPS RAM ceiling** — You're at 2 GB soft limit. Email service + analytics are lightweight, but don't add heavy background jobs (e.g., batch translation re-runs) without monitoring `htop`.
3. **DMCA response time** — You're legally required to act "expeditiously" on takedown requests (typically interpreted as 24–48 hours). Make sure you check the admin Reports tab daily once the form goes live.
4. **Analytics GDPR** — Plausible/Fathom are cookieless and GDPR-exempt; Google Analytics requires a cookie banner. Stick with privacy-first tools unless you add explicit consent UI.
