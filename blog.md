# SingoLing Blog

## Week 1 — Learn a Language by Falling in Love with a Song (May 3–9, 2026)

Welcome to **SingoLing** — a new way to learn a language through the music you already love. Pick a song, press play, and watch the lyrics scroll in perfect sync. Every word is one tap away from its meaning, its grammar, and how it's used. No flashcards. No drills. Just you, the song, and the language meeting you where you are.

We launched this week with a clean dark-mode design, smooth transitions between songs, and visuals that let every track set its own mood. This is just the first verse — we can't wait for you to start singing along.

## Week 2 — Speak Your Language, Sing Theirs (May 10–16, 2026)

This week was about meeting more of you. We added quick sign-in options, sorted our library into **Beginner, Intermediate, and Advanced** playlists, and stocked every tier with songs worth putting on repeat.

The bigger headline: SingoLing is now **truly multilingual on both sides of the screen**. You can study across multiple target languages, and the app itself now speaks your language too. Found a translation that doesn't quite fit? Tap to let us know — we read every one. We also polished the player, sped things up across the board, and gave the Settings page a refresh. More songs and more surprises next week. Keep singing.
