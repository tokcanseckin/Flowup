# SingoLing Blog

## Week 1 — Learn a Language by Falling in Love with a Song (May 3–9, 2026)

Welcome to **SingoLing** — a new way to learn a language through the music you already love. This week we put the very first version of the app into the world. Pick a song, press play on **YouTube** or **Apple Music**, and watch the lyrics scroll in perfect sync with the recording. Every word is one tap (or one keystroke) away from revealing its meaning, its grammar, and how it's used. No flashcards. No drills. Just you, the song, and the language meeting you where you are.

We launched at **singoling.com** with a clean dark-mode design, smooth song-to-song transitions, and album-art-driven backgrounds that make every track feel like its own little world. We also built the engine that quietly powers it all behind the scenes — so the lyrics show up the instant the music starts and your favorite song is never more than a click away from becoming a lesson.

This is just the first verse. We can't wait for you to start singing along.

## Week 2 — Speak Your Language, Sing Theirs (May 10–16, 2026)

This week was all about meeting more of you. We added **Google and Apple Sign In** with fresh login and signup pages, sorted our playlists by **Beginner, Intermediate, and Advanced** so you can pick a level that fits, and stocked the library with curated playlists at every tier. Whether you're easing in or already humming along, there's something to put on repeat.

The bigger headline: SingoLing is now **truly multilingual on both sides of the screen**. You can study in multiple target languages, and the interface itself speaks **English, Turkish, and Russian** — including the grammar labels in the word-inspect panel, so the whole experience feels native no matter who you are. Found a translation that doesn't quite fit? Just tap the new **"Report a problem"** button right in the lyrics and let us know — we read every one.

We also gave the player a round of polish, made song processing dramatically faster so new music lands sooner, and refreshed the Settings page with a preferred-source picker, a pause-on-inspect toggle, and a Support tab that lets you message us directly. More languages, more songs, and more surprises are queued up for next week. Keep singing.
